Why you should choose our services

What makes Fresh Scents different from other car air freshener companies?
Fresh Scents is unique because we are the only car air freshener company in the world that uses perfume-based scents. Our fragrances are developed in collaboration with scientists, ensuring a high-quality and distinctive product.
How many custom car air fresheners does Fresh Scents produce each month?

We produce 10,000,000 custom car air fresheners every month in our 1000 m² factory.

What materials do you use to make your car air fresheners?

We use top-quality materials to manufacture our car air fresheners, ensuring they meet the highest standards in the industry.

How long does the fragrance last in Fresh Scents air fresheners?
The longevity of our fragrances can vary based on environmental conditions, but typically, our air fresheners maintain their scent for several weeks.
Can I customize the scent of my car air freshener?

Yes, we offer customization options for our clients, allowing them to choose from a variety of perfume-based scents to match their preferences.

What is the minimum order quantity for custom air fresheners?

The minimum order quantity varies based on the customization requirements. Please contact our sales team for specific details regarding your order.
